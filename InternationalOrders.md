## Payment methods

### PayPal

PayPal is the preferred payment method for most international retail orders.

After the order is confirmed, I will send a PayPal invoice in USD. Please make sure the shipping address shown in the PayPal transaction is complete and correct.

The order will be shipped only to the address associated with the confirmed payment unless otherwise agreed in writing before payment.

### Bank transfer to BOCHK

Bank transfer to a Bank of China (Hong Kong) account is available on request.

Bank account details will be provided privately after the order has been confirmed. They will not be published on this page.

The buyer is responsible for all sending-bank, intermediary-bank, correspondent-bank, and currency-conversion charges. The order will be processed only after the full invoiced amount has been credited to the receiving account.

For small retail orders, PayPal is usually simpler and more economical than an international SWIFT transfer.
